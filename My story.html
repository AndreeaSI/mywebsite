My story...

Hi there! I’m Andreea and I started my tech journey about 9 months ago. The idea of getting into IT was like a lightning strike to me and it made so much sense so I start learning a programming language. My first (and favourite!) language I dived in was Java Script. Managing to write my first blocks of code on my own, by simply watching a tutorial on udemy.com, was so rewarding to me. After a long time, I finally felt creative and on the right track I would say, since my day job as retail cashier did not give me anything but emptiness.
Technology incorporates so many things we surround ourselves with every day of this modern world, is truly improving our lives so I find this a fascinating time to join this field. I do have some soft spots for other things than learning programming languages, one of them would be aesthetic design or what I call signature style (a way you choose to express yourself through everything you surround yourself with from fabrics to home design). It’s a territory where I seek for balance where style never overcomes environmental awareness. I work towards a more sustainable future (on a very small scale, I’m aware) and by making smarter choices I am limiting my personal waste, and thanks to many very talented artists how also embraced this movement, I do not have to compromise my sense of styling in any way.
Technology can take a huge part in a sustainable development process that benefits both us and future generations who also deserve a close to nature living, so my goal is to build upon my software programming knowledge and implement it in projects that sustain an ecologically friendly environment.
